CPVC Pipes – Durable & Heat-Resistant

Strong, long-lasting, and safe piping solutions for homes and industries.

Overview

CPVC (Chlorinated Polyvinyl Chloride) pipes are known for their exceptional durability, heat resistance, and corrosion-free properties. Designed to withstand high temperatures and chemical exposure, CPVC pipes are widely used for hot and cold water supply, industrial applications, and plumbing systems in residential and commercial projects.

Usages

  • Residential plumbing systems (hot & cold water supply)
  • Industrial and chemical piping
  • Fire sprinkler systems
  • Commercial buildings and municipal water lines

Advantages

  • Long lifespan and corrosion-resistant
  • Lightweight and easy to install
  • Leak-proof joints with strong fittings
  • Safe for drinking water
  • Cost-effective piping solution
  • Withstands high temperatures
  • Eco-friendly and low maintenance
  • Complies with international standards
